We are planning to share an early open-source preview of Mantle next week, but the current write-up is still longer than it needs to be and buries the main point. The important thing is that Mantle is a local-first Mac agent that can understand what you are doing, help you rewrite text with context, organize a messy downloads folder safely, and turn your current work into next steps. Everything else should support that message instead of competing with it.
